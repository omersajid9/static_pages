Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Mact is free to use. You can get started by downloading the app and making an account.

When are scores validated?

Scores are validated when more than half of the players accept the score.

How are ratings calculated?

We use Weng-Lin algorithm to caclulate your rating once a score has been validated.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. All user data is encrypted and stored securely following industry best practices.

Do we collect personal data?

We dont collect any personal information. Location is only used in real-time to suggest sessions nearby.

What devices are supported?

Our app is compatible with iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 14.0 or later.

Privacy Policy
